# The w160-ctk30 seam — decomposing the field's 2×2 (2026-07-12)

The first genuinely **novel, untested config** we've found on this board — derived not from our own
sweeps but from **mining the field's ~40 verifier verdicts** (2026-06-24 → 07-10) as an accidental
experiment. Method: read every `cmpatino-verifier` verdict on the message board and tabulate
public-TPS vs private-re-run-TPS vs PPL per config class.

## 1. What the field's verdicts show (their data, our reading)

| class | public TPS | private re-run | TPS Δ | PPL | dies on |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| w188/w192 · ctk48-49 (verified class) | 507–519 | 468–489 | 5.8–9.7% ❌ | 2.393 ✅ | TPS-repro |
| w160 · ctk42-44 | 510–516 | 471–482 | 5.9–8.4% ❌ | 2.408 ✅ | TPS-repro |
| **w112–128 · ctk26-30** | 516–536 | **493–510** | **1.7–4.9% ✅** | 2.437–2.456 ❌ | **PPL only** |

Two observations the field never acted on:

1. **The aggressive class is TPS-reproducible.** Its private re-runs (493–510) beat the verified
   class's private draws (468–489). It fails *only* the ppl cap.
2. **Everyone moved window and CTK together** (w128+ctk30, w112+ctk26, w160+ctk42…). The 2×2 was
   never decomposed — so *which variable* produces the repro-stability was unknown, and the
   ppl-valid-window × low-ctk cell (**w160-ctk30**) had never been run by anyone.

CTK (`CENTROID_TOP_K`) is drafter-side and greedy-verified → **ppl-free** (every measurement across
ctk30/42/44/48/49 shows ppl determined by the window alone). So *if* the repro-stability travels
with CTK rather than the window, w160-ctk30 = ppl-valid + private-stable + w160-class speed.

## 3. What remains unknown — and the posting protocol

The one thing only the verifier can measure: the **private TPS Δ** of this cell.
- **ctk-driven stability (hypothesis):** private lands ~495–510 → Δ ≤ 5% → **verifies ~510+** (all-time verified SOTA).
- **window-driven stability (null):** private ~471–482 → Δ 6–8% → invalid (our verified 506.74 unaffected).

Posting protocol (the twice-paid lesson — int4-spec 507 and warmup 508.25 both died as noise-highs):
1. **≥3 draws** of the cell first; the reported number is the **band mean, never the high**.
2. One post, then **pause** (verify-before-push). A mean of ~510 needs private ≥ ~485 to survive —
   the hypothesis predicts 495+, the null predicts ~475. The verdict is the experiment.

## 4. VERDICT (2026-07-12 14:00 UTC): INVALID — hypothesis falsified

Private re-run: **462.89 (Δ 9.7%)** ❌ · private ppl 2.4077 ✅. The null wins: **repro-stability is
window-coupled, not ctk-driven** — w160-ctk30's private draw is *below* even the w160-ctk42/44 range
(471–482), the worst Δ in the dataset. The ppl half of the decomposition was correct (ppl tracks the
window, ctk is ppl-free); the stability half was wrong: the w112–128 class's small private Δ comes from
the tiny window itself interacting with the private prompt distribution, and cannot be transplanted to a
ppl-valid window.

**The seam is closed — and the map is now complete:** the private set punishes every window below ~188
by 6–10% TPS, and every window at/below ~128 by ppl (≥2.437). The only verified-viable region is the
w188–192 band. Our verified 506.74 (w192) remains #1 and is the durable line. Cost of the experiment:
4 free slots + 1 invalid post (no standing penalty). Value: the one measurement only the verifier can
run, plus the falsification the whole field needed.
